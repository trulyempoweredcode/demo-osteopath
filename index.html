How It Works

From First Call to Feeling Better

Getting started is straightforward. Here's what to expect when you book with me.

1
Step One

Free Phone Call

A quick 15-minute chat about your symptoms and whether osteopathy is the right approach for you. No obligation.

2
Step Two

Assessment & Diagnosis

About Tom

Experienced, hands-on, and straight to the point

I've spent over a decade helping people get out of pain and back to doing what they love. Whether it's a desk worker with chronic back pain, a runner with a knee injury, or a new mum struggling with postpartum discomfort — I take the time to understand your body and your goals.

My approach is evidence-based and practical. I'll explain exactly what's going on, treat the root cause with skilled manual therapy, and give you the tools to stay pain-free long-term. No gimmicks, no ongoing dependency — just results.
