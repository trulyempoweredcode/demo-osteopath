Welcome

A straightforward, hands-on approach to pain relief

Osteopathy isn't just about cracking backs. It's a thorough, whole-body approach to understanding why you're in pain and what's causing it. I use a combination of soft tissue work, joint mobilisation, and manipulation to treat the root of the problem — not just manage the symptoms.

Whether you've woken up with a stiff neck, you're recovering from a sports injury, or you've been putting up with back pain for months, I can help you get moving again. Most patients notice a real difference within the first two or three sessions.

Osteopathy is a regulated healthcare profession — not alternative therapy.

Every osteopath in the UK is registered with the General Osteopathic Council and has completed a minimum of four years' full-time university training. We study anatomy, physiology, pathology, and clinical methods alongside hands-on technique. That means you're being treated by someone who understands how the whole body works, can recognise when something needs further investigation, and knows exactly how to help your body heal itself more effectively.
