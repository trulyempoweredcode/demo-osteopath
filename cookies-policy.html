Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and supply information to the website owner. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information unless you have provided it to the site.

2. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of this website. They enable core features such as remembering your cookie consent preference. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

3. Analytics Cookies

I may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with this website. These cookies collect information anonymously and help me improve the site by understanding which pages are visited most often, how long visitors spend on each page, and how they arrived at the site.

Analytics cookies are only set after you accept cookies via the consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies will be placed on your device.

4. Third-Party Cookies

This website may embed content from third-party services such as Google Maps. These services may set their own cookies, which are governed by the respective third party's privacy and cookie policies. I do not have control over these cookies.

  • Google Maps: used on the contact page to show the location of my practice. Google may set cookies to enable the map functionality. See Google's privacy policy for details.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can typically choose to block all cookies, accept all cookies, or be notified when a cookie is set. The process varies by browser — check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Cookie consent banner: when you first visit this website, a consent banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Opt-out tools: you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

6. Changes to This Policy

I may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or my business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.
