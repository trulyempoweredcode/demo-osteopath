What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owner. Cookies help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies We Use

This website uses only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the site to function. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

The essential cookies used on this site are:

  • Cookie consent preference — Stores whether you have accepted or dismissed the cookie banner. This prevents the banner from appearing on every page visit. This cookie expires after 365 days.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to block or delete cookies, though this may affect how certain websites function. Below are links to cookie management instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences > Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Third-Party Cookies

This website embeds a Google Maps iframe on the contact page. Google may set its own cookies when you interact with the map. These cookies are governed by Google's own privacy and cookie policies, which you can review on the Google Privacy & Terms page. We do not control these third-party cookies.

Apart from the Google Maps embed, this website does not use any third-party services that set cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to revisit this page periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
